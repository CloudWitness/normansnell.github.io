About Norman

Norman Snell is a teacher and author based in New Jersey. His work focuses on helping people read Scripture on their own terms — without the noise, the guilt, or the frameworks that get in the way.

Through his writing and his ongoing project Faith and History, Norman invites readers to encounter the Bible as it was meant to be encountered: honestly, deeply, and without pretense.

Want to go deeper?

Faith and History is Norman's ongoing exploration of how the story of Scripture connects to real history, real geography, and real people. It's the Bible without the filter.
